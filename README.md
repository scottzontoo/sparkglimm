# SparkGlim.net - Ghana's Leading Digital Voting, Ticketing, Fundraising, and USSD Platform

SparkGlim is an all-in-one platform for **online voting campaigns**, **event ticketing**, **digital fundraising**, and **USSD-based transactions** in Ghana.  
It helps organizers launch campaigns quickly, collect payments securely, and track results in real time.

If you run awards, elections, pageants, conferences, concerts, student programs, or donation drives, SparkGlim gives you the tools to launch, grow, and monetize with less operational friction.

Website: [https://sparkglim.net](https://sparkglim.net)

### USSD Service (No-Internet Access)

SparkGlim extends digital access through USSD for users without smartphone data access.

- Main USSD access code: `*928*918#`
- Works across major Ghana networks
- Supports ticket purchase, voting, and donation use cases
- Designed for secure and simple guided sessions
- Includes anti-abuse and transaction protection controls

USSD is a major advantage for high-reach campaigns targeting both urban and lower-connectivity regions.

## How SparkGlim Works

For organizers, the core flow is simple:

1. Create a campaign (voting, ticketing, or fundraising).
2. Share your campaign link with your audience.
3. Monitor live performance and withdraw funds to bank or mobile wallet.

For participants:

1. Open campaign link or use USSD.
2. Choose nominee/ticket/cause.
3. Pay via preferred method and receive instant confirmation.

## Pricing and Revenue Model
